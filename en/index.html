Kanji-zushi is a game where players combine neta cards from their hand (the left halves) with shari cards on the table (the right halves) to complete valid kanji characters.

Download PDF Back it on Kickstarter

Shari cards

Each of these rice-themed cards has the right half of a kanji, and you will find them on the table.

Neta cards

Each of these cards themed as sushi toppings has the left half of a kanji. You will have some of these in your hand.

Kanji made from two cards

Add the number of strokes on both cards to your score.
